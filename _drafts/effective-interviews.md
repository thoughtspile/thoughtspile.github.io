## The tech interview

My basic routine for a 90-minute tech interview is as follows:

1. Ice-breaker.
2. Team & product intro.
3. Live coding.
5. Ample time for reverse interview.

I know some people hate the _idea_ of live coding in an interview. Well, so be it.
